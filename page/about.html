OUR BUSINESS INSIGHTS

Learn more about our strategy, products, and customers.

BUSINESS OVERVIEW

Our business is in the clothing industry.
We focus on selling fashionable and affordable clothes.
The business mainly sells products online, using social media and marketplaces.

TARGET CUSTOMERS

Our target customers are young people aged 18 to 35.
They like trendy, comfortable, and affordable clothes.
Understanding customers helps the business design the right products and marketing.

MARKETING STRATEGY

We use social media marketing such as Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok.
We also work with influencers and post videos showing how to wear our clothes.
Marketing helps increase brand awareness and sales.

CUSTOMER SERVICE

Good customer service is very important.
We reply quickly to customers, allow easy exchanges, and communicate politely.
Happy customers are more likely to buy again.
